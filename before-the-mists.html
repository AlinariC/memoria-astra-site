Before the Mists

Book Two in the Memoria Astra Cycle

The Lost Cradle of Venus

Long before Terra’s mists thickened into memory, another world breathed its last. On Venus — once lush, once teeming — a dying civilization faced its final twilight.

Driven by desperation, wonder, and betrayal, the Phorsians launched their final exodus into the darkness, hoping to find refuge among the stars. Their journey would seed myths, ignite legends, and forever alter the fate of distant worlds yet unborn.

Before the Mists is the prelude to humanity’s forgotten heritage — a story of collapse, courage, and the ancient promises woven deep into the Spiral’s breath.
