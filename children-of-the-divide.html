Children of the Divide

Jupiter’s Legacy, Saturn’s Reckoning

Between the swirling storms of Jupiter and the icy rings of Saturn, a divided generation arises—born in the shadow of titanic forces, scattered across moons and habitats.

“They were not born of Earth, nor of any single world. They were born of the breach—and from that breach, they will decide our fate.”

*Children of the Divide* follows a fractured lineage of settlers, pioneers, and rebels—descendants of those who fled the collapse on Mars. Now, amid floating metropolises and storm-hardened citadels, a new conflict brews between unity and isolation.

A celestial oracle speaks. An ancient divide is challenged. And from the shadows of forgotten outposts, something older than stars begins to whisper again.
