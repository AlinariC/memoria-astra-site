Children of the Divide

Book Four in the Memoria Astra Cycle

Paths Forked Beneath Alien Skies

The seeds scattered from ancient worlds have taken root across distant skies — but not all growth leads toward unity. Across moons, lost stations, and fractured colonies, humanity splinters: cultures diverge, memories fade, and a silent rift deepens across the breathing arms of the Spiral.

In the Divide, hope still flickers. In the Divide, despair sharpens. In the Divide, the first threads of an ancient sorrow begin weaving themselves into the fabric of destiny once more.

Children of the Divide is a story of separation, resilience, and the delicate fracture lines that will shape — and break — the future to come.
