Crimson Reliquary

Empire Among the Ruins

Mars—the red world—was not always barren. In the blood-soaked dust lie the shattered remnants of a once-great civilization that sought to wield the power of the stars.

“Beneath crimson sands, the reliquary weeps. Not for the dead, but for what they dared awaken.”

*Crimson Reliquary* follows an expedition of skyship survivors and cosmic archaeologists as they delve into Martian ruins that pulse with residual energy and ancient codes. Amid obsidian monoliths and red-lit catacombs, the truth of a vanished empire begins to stir once more.

What they find is not just history—it is prophecy. A memory machine buried deep within Olympus Mons. A weapon, or a message. A second chance, or final judgment.

Back Cover Blurb:
Among rust and ruin, the past calls out. Will we answer with wonder—or war?
