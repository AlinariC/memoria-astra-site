Echoes of Lyra

Book Five in the Memoria Astra Cycle

The Last Song Before the Fall

As the First Spiral collapses, as memory unravels into starlight and dust, a single signal cuts across the dying heavens — a last whisper of hope, sorrow, and warning.

In the ruins of lost worlds and the fading breaths of forgotten empires, the Echoes of Lyra weave themselves into Wells, Looms, and the secret cradles of what might still be reborn.

Echoes of Lyra is a story of endings and beginnings, a love letter to all that once was, and a heartbeat carried forward into the silence that must one day bloom anew.
