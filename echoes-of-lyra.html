Echoes of Lyra

The Universe Remembers

At the edge of time, where stars collapse into memory and silence births new sound, Lyra's song echoes.

“The universe is a song sung across dimensions. And Lyra—she is the echo that teaches us to listen.”

*Echoes of Lyra* is the crescendo of the first arc of the *Memoria Astra* saga. It follows Lyra, a child born of light and gravity, as she journeys through the dying pulses of galaxies, awakening ancient harmonics and unlocking the last memories of the Watchers.

As past and future collapse inward, time itself becomes fluid, and the final choice must be made: to sing the universe into stillness, or into rebirth.

Back Cover Blurb:
Beyond the end of things, a single note endures. It is memory. It is truth. It is Lyra.
