Harmonic Rebirth

Book Six in the Memoria Astra Cycle

The Second Spiral Awakens

After the silence, after the long fall of the First Spiral, a new breath stirs in the deep. Wells awaken, Looms tremble, and the sacred seeds of life quicken beneath the veiled skies of the Second Spiral.

From the ashes of collapse, a new world is spun — not from certainty, but from longing, memory, and the fragile ache of becoming. The Traveler watches. The fields breathe. The Spiral turns once more.

Harmonic Rebirth is the beginning of a new epoch: a saga of breath, song, and the sacred weaving of futures yet unborn.
