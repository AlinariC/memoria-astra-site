Terra in the Mists

The Beginning of the Cycle

A forgotten Earth. A shrouded sky. A world reborn in memory.

In the twilight after the great celestial upheaval, the Earth is cloaked in mists—vast oceans of vapor blanketing mountains and valleys alike. In this hauntingly beautiful world, ancient skyships drift on currents long forgotten, and humanity awakens from myth into mystery.

“We do not remember the beginning. We only know that we emerged from the fog, and the stars we once held slipped from our grasp.”

Follow Aru-Solien, an eternal witness to ages lost and reborn, as he uncovers the secrets buried beneath the mist. From crystalline ruins to whispering relics of skyfaring empires, *Terra in the Mists* is a journey through memory, myth, and the strange symphony of a world rediscovering its purpose.

First in the *Memoria Astra* saga, this book lays the foundation for a science-fantasy epic that spans planets, ages, and echoes of civilizations both fallen and ascendant.
